## Overview

Sophisticated and serene, with a premium spa-like quality that emphasizes natural luxury. The design feels spacious and uncluttered, with a refined European sensibility that conveys trust and expertise in natural skincare.

Generous whitespace and asymmetrical balance define the layout approach. Content is organized in clear sections with ample breathing room, using a flexible grid system that accommodates both text-heavy and image-focused content areas.

Uses a 4px base grid with scale: 1, 2, 4, 5, 10, 15.

## Colors
- **Charcoal Stone** (#1e1e1e): Primary text color, navigation, and headings
- **Pure White** (#ffffff): Main background, cards, and content areas
- **Alpine Mist** (#f2eeeb): Secondary background, subtle sections
- **True Black** (#000000): High contrast text and accents
- **Light Gray** (#e6e6e6): Subtle borders and dividers
- **Mountain Berry** (#4d384b): Interactive elements and brand accents

## Typography
- **Headline Font**: Martina Plantijn
- **Body Font**: KaufmannSans
- **Label Font**: KaufmannSans

The typography system creates elegant contrast through the pairing of a refined serif (Martina Plantijn) for headlines with a clean, modern sans-serif (KaufmannSans) for body text. Font weights remain consistently light to medium, with hierarchy established through size relationships rather than bold weights. Letter-spacing is carefully tuned across all sizes, with slightly increased tracking on smaller text for improved readability.

## Elevation

The design employs minimal shadows and relies primarily on subtle color contrast and generous whitespace to create hierarchy. Depth is achieved through layered imagery and content overlays rather than explicit shadow systems, maintaining a flat but sophisticated aesthetic.

## Components
- **Hero Section**: Split-screen layout with large imagery on left and content overlay on right, featuring large serif headings and minimal button styling
- **Navigation Bar**: Clean horizontal navigation with brand logo, category links, and utility icons aligned to edges with generous spacing
- **Product Card**: Minimal product presentation with clean imagery, subtle borders, and restrained typography hierarchy
- **Call-to-Action Button**: Outlined button style with thin borders, no background fill, maintaining the minimal aesthetic
- **Content Overlay**: Text content overlaid on imagery with semi-transparent backgrounds for readability while maintaining visual impact

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do maintain generous whitespace to preserve the premium, uncluttered aesthetic
- Don't use heavy font weights - keep typography light and elegant
- Do use the warm neutral (#f2eeeb) sparingly as a subtle background accent
- Don't introduce bright or saturated colors that would disrupt the natural palette
- Do ensure sufficient contrast ratios with the dark text colors
- Don't overcrowd layouts - embrace the minimalist spacing system
- Do use the serif font (Martina Plantijn) exclusively for headlines and key messaging
- Don't mix additional font families - the two-font system is intentionally restrained
