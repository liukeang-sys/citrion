The Project ICBSC 2025
Overview

A brand built from scratch.

Citrion is a fictional clean beauty brand developed from the ground up as part of the ICBSC — International Collegiate Business Strategy Competition, organized by the UCR Anderson Graduate School of Management (UCR AGSM).

The brief called for a complete go-to-market strategy: brand identity, target market analysis, pricing model, digital marketing plan, and a live product website. Citrion was the answer — a peptide-infused tinted lip balm positioned at the intersection of clean beauty and accessible luxury.

Every element of this site — from the product copy to the SEO structure — was intentionally designed and built as part of the competition deliverable.

Strategy Overview
Step 01

Market & Consumer Research

Identified a gap in the clean beauty lip care segment — consumers wanted both performance and ingredient transparency. Citrion was positioned to own that space.

Step 02

Brand Identity & Product Development

Developed the Citrion name, visual language, and product line — four shades of the Glaze peptide tinted lip balm at an accessible $18 price point.

Step 03

Digital Presence & SEO

Built a fully functional product site with on-page SEO, schema markup, Google Analytics integration, and Google Search Console configuration — treated as a real launch.

Step 04

Go-to-Market Strategy

Outlined a phased GTM plan covering organic search, social content, and a retail partnership framework — benchmarked against comparable clean beauty brands.
