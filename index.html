The Brand

Effortless confidence, thoughtfully made.

Citrion was born from a belief that beauty and performance are not in conflict. Each product is formulated to deliver real results — peptide-infused, botanically-anchored, designed for every skin tone and every day.

The brand was developed as a complete go-to-market strategy: identity, pricing, digital marketing, and a live product website — built as if it were a real launch.
